About - What inspired the creation of this project


So as with many young adolescents, from childhood I had neglected the practise of building good, strong habits that encouraged the development of my personal growth and self care. I instead directed most of my time and attention towards easier methods of enjoyment such as video games and binge watching TV shows. I had not been instilled with much responsibility, discipline and the ability to control my continuous influx of thoughts which gradually increased as I aged and become extremely difficult to manage and control especially when I had unintentionally, developed bad habits and a weak-willed mindset which casually, always prioritised ease and pleasure over confronting and overcoming the challenge at hand.


It sent me into a cycle of emptiness and depression that continued to haunt me in my high school years. That is, until I found the concept of "self-improvement". Trying to continually develop and live as the best version of myself and cut that image of me who indulged in a worthless activities that encouraged my decline instead of my growth.


In order to consistently be that better version of myself and achieve the lifestyle I desired, I needed to start living a more structured and disciplined life. And instead of consistently reflecting and designing that lifestyle on a paper, I decided to implement my developing skills as a Software Engineer and build a desktop application to do just that.
